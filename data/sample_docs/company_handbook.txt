ACME CORP — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (SAMPLE DOCUMENT)

1. REFUND POLICY
Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided the product is
returned in its original condition. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the
original payment method. Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded.

2. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval. Employees
must be reachable on Slack during core hours (10 AM – 4 PM local time).

3. VACATION POLICY
Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days of paid vacation per month, capped at 20 days per year.
Unused vacation days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days into the following year.

4. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Employees can submit expense reports for approved business expenses within 60 days of the
expense date. Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll cycle after approval.

5. ONBOARDING PROCESS
New employees complete a 2-week onboarding program that includes company orientation, tool
setup, and a mentorship pairing with a senior team member for the first 90 days.
